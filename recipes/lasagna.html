Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna may have a few steps, but each step is easy – and I assure you it’s worth the time; the perfect Italian meal! This is a really easy lasagna recipe.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna Noodles
  • 1/2 Pound Lean Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 36 Ounces Tomato Sauce
  • 2 Cloves Garlic Minced
  • 1 Teaspoon Italian Seasoning
  • 2 Cups Ricotta Cheese
  • 1 Egg Beaten

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In a large bot of boiling water, add the lasgna noodles and cook for 9 minutes
  3. In a skillet, brown the lean ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat
  4. Stir in the pasta sauce and Italian seasoning
  5. Combine the cheese and egg
  6. Layer noodles with the filling and then top with the cheese
  7. Bake for 45 minutes

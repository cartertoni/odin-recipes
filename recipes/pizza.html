Pizza

Description

Canadian bacon is a classic pizza topping. Combine it with fresh mushrooms and you've got a supreme pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pizza Crust
  • Can of Pizza Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • 6 Mushrooms
  • Crumbled Bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450
  2. Spread pizza sauce on crust
  3. Spread pepperoni, diced mushrooms, and crumbled bacon evenly across pizza
  4. Place directly on oven rack and cook for 15 minutes
  5. Remove, let cool momentarily, and slice into eighths

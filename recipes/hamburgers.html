Hamburger

Description

Big juicy hamburger patties don’t need a lot of ingredients or extras. With just a few great tips, these patties are perfect for cookouts all year ’round!

Ingredients

  • 1 Bun
  • 1 Hamburger Patty
  • 1 Slice of Tomato
  • 2 Pickles
  • 1 Piece of Lettuce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet to medium heat
  2. Cook burger in skillet for approximately 6 minutes per side, depending on thickness of patty
  3. While burger is cooking, slice tomato and pickles
  4. Spread mayonnaise on bottom bun
  5. Once burger is cooked, place patty on bottom bun and add pickles, ketchup, mustard, tomato, and lettuce
